BLOBi

My latest game, BLOBi, was coded in JavaScript. You are BLOBi, a little green blob. The objective of the game is to try to eat smaller blobs and gain size to be the largest blob. Be careful -- if you touch a larger blob, it will eat YOU. Control BLOBi using the up and down arrow keys. Hope you enjoy!
